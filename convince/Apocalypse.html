    What is it?

    Avant Apocalypse is a fashion aesthetic characterised by neutral maximalism and wearing pieces incorrectly. The aesthetic contains elements of Plaguecore and Post-Apocalyptic, as well as upcycling. You can also see it as a fashion style featuring Y2K+Cyber Punk+Subversive Basics!


    Popular Visual Cues to look out for

    • Layers
    • Sheer Fabric
    • Unconventional Sillhouettes
    • Deconstruction
    • Neutral Tones

    WHY NOT PREPARE FOR THE APOCALYPSE AHEAD OF TIME IN CASE IT DOES COME? You would be ahead of the game. In fact, the whole government in terms of preparing for the end of the world.


    The Problems

    Honestly, Avant Apocalypse is not a very convinent or comfortable aesthetic. It is not something that you will see often because it is a bit extreme. This aesthetic may not be for everyone. It is basically "put-together messy" with clashing and unusual pieces. I wouldn't be suprised if someone thought this was pure craziness. I thought this was crazy too at first. "Who prepares for what they would wear for the apocalypse?" But look at how nice these pieces lookkkk~
