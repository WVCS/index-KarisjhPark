    What is it?

    Avant Apocalypse is a fashion aesthetic characterised by neutral maximalism and wearing pieces incorrectly. The aesthetic contains elements of Plaguecore and Post-Apocalyptic, as well as upcycling. You can also see it as a fashion style featuring Y2K+Cyber Punk+Subversive Basics!
