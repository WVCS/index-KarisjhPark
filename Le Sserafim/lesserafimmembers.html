    Le Sserafim (르세라핌) is a 5-member girl group under HYBE and Source Music. LE SSERAFIM debuted on May 2, 2022 with their 1st mini album FEARLESS.
    The name "LE SSERAFIM" is an anagram of the phrase "I'm Fearless";
    it implies self-confidence and strong will to move forward without being swayed by the world's gaze. It also references Seraphim, a six-winged heavenly creature, referencing the group's original six members.
